Druig

The Eternal

Biography

Druig is a member of the Eternals, an immortal alien race created by the Celestials. Known for his enigmatic personality and exceptional powers, Druig often finds himself in moral conflicts regarding the use of his abilities to influence humanity.

Powers and Abilities

  • Telepathy: Druig can manipulate the minds of others, allowing him to control their actions.
  • Illusions: He can create realistic illusions, affecting what others perceive.
  • Immortality: Like all Eternals, Druig does not age and can heal from almost any injury.
  • Superhuman Strength: He possesses enhanced strength, agility, and durability compared to humans.

Affiliations

Druig is part of the Eternals, often interacting with other members such as Sersi, Ikaris, and Thena. He has a complicated relationship with humanity and the other Eternals due to his differing views on intervention.
