Biography

Solar Sentinel is a guardian of the Earth, harnessing the power of the sun to protect humanity. With the ability to manipulate solar energy, they can create blinding flashes of light and powerful energy blasts. Their mission is to combat darkness and bring hope to those in need.

Powers and Abilities

  • Solar Energy Manipulation: Can absorb and project solar energy as blasts or barriers.
  • Flight: Leverages solar power to soar through the skies at incredible speeds.
  • Healing: Can heal wounds by channeling sunlight, accelerating recovery for themselves and others.
